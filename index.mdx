Rose gives you access to every skin in League of Legends — for free. It runs silently in your system tray, detects your skin selection during champion select, and injects the chosen skin when the game starts. Built on the [Pengu Loader](https://github.com/Tariolle/ROSE-Pengu) framework, Rose modifies only local rendering variables and never touches network data, memory states, or gameplay mechanics.

## How Rose works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download and install">
    Grab the latest installer from [GitHub Releases](https://github.com/Alban1911/Rose/releases/latest) and run it as Administrator. Rose installs itself and registers a Start Menu shortcut.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Provide the injection DLL">
    On first launch, Rose prompts you for a signed injection DLL. This file cannot be bundled due to DMCA restrictions — you must supply your own from an authorized source.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Launch Rose before League">
    Start Rose from the Start Menu or system tray. It activates Pengu Loader automatically and waits for the League Client to open.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick your skin in champion select">
    Hover over any skin in champion select. Rose detects your selection, lets you choose chromas or alternate forms, and injects the skin when the game loads.
  </Step>
</Steps>
